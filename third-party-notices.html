MorphBrain atlas reference

The GW21–36 playback uses weekly white-matter surfaces derived from the Developing Human Connectome Project spatio-temporal surface atlas of the fetal brain. The source atlas is a population-average reference released under the CC0 1.0 Public Domain Dedication; it is not subject MRI and is not the molecular model developing.

Source: Karolis et al., Developing Human Connectome Project spatio-temporal surface atlas of the fetal brain.

Model Morpher recording

The recorded DG-to-root example uses Allen Mouse Brain Common Coordinate Framework meshes delivered by BrainGlobe as allen_mouse_25um v1.2. Image/data credit: Allen Institute. This is a noncommercial research demonstration.
